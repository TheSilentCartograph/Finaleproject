What Is It?

Do you ever notice how cheap meat can get? How is it possible to supply so much meat to so many people and do it so inexpensively? One of the reasons are Concentrated Animal Feeding Operations or CAFOs for short. They house thousands of animals in small spaces to maximixe profits.

Six CAFOs in Illinois

The map to the left showcases six CAFOs within Illinois and how much waste they produce and their proximity to major bodies of water. If you zoom in on them you might notice a pattern on how they are constructed. They all have lakes,lagoons, and or pools in order to contain the millions of gallons of waste produced per year.
